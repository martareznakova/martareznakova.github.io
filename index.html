About me

 

I am rather curious, adventurous and smiley person. I like to explore and experience new things, places, events, tastes, smells, people, everything. I also like things to be neat and in order, which is a reason why I tend to dig through and find out how things work. And so I do Machine Learning. Perhaps I like to be the puppeteer and perhaps it is because I like to find the system, the pattern in behaviors. In my opinion, once you find the pattern, you can wrap it in some model or formula. And this search is very much rewarding. Nevertheless, some things I prefer chaotic, like nature. I like to go hiking, swimming outdoors, horseback riding, ... I really love fruits, my favorite icecream is vanilla and pistachio, and I do not like coffee (just in case ..).
